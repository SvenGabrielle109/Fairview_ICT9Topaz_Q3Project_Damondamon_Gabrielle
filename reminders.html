Reminders

  1. Make sure you kept your ticket safe with you upon arriving and departing.
  2. Be on time before the train departs.
  3. Pay the counter for your ticket.
  4. Be attentive to your belongings.
  5. Pay attention to the annoucement ALL THE TIME!
  6. Be attentive to your children to avoid accidents/incidents.
  7. Always have your phone with you in case of train reschedule or train cancellation.
  8. PLEASE keep the lobby clean as possible!
  9. Always respect the staff members and the guards
  10. Follow the protocols inside the train.
  11. Put on put your phone on SILENT MODE.


Tips

  1. You can pay the counter either by card, by online cash, or by cash.
  2. In case you pay your ticker online, you can show an online or a printed copy of the ticket. Or, you can scan your OR code of the ticket.
  3. You can arrive to the train station early before the expected time of the departure.
  4. You have to charge your phone before arriving to the train to save enough battery for paying at the counter.
  5. While waiting for the train to arrive, you can try sleep, eat some snacks or meals, go shopping to various shops in the waiting area, and/or do some internet stuff (for as long as the volume is low ).
