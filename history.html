Our History



When did it all start?

It all began with Eddie Eggplant, a determined farmer known for his delicously breeded eggplants. One fateful afternoon, Eddie had to rush a batch of fresh eggplants to his friend’s house. The problem? The nearest train station was a 2 hour walk away! Frustrated but determined, Eddie knew there had to be a better way.

Instead of accepting the 2 hour walk, Eddie did the unthinkable— he built his own railway. With sheer determination, a few helping hands, and a vision for convenience, the first tracks of what would later become the Eggplant Express were laid.

Our time in the present

Today, Eddie’s once-small railway has grown into a fully operational transit system, helping people (and eggplants) travel quickly and efficiently. The Eggplant Express is now a staple in the community, offering a reliable and quick way to get around. What started as one farmer’s struggle has become a beloved railway for everyone.
